About Me:

I am from Buffalo, New York and am currently working as an eighth grade English teacher. I live in the city with my husband, our 2 month old daughter, Frankie, and our two Alaskan huskies, Sunny and Luna. I love just going for walks around the city or going on hikes when the weather is nice. I'm an avid reader and recently am getting more into fantasy novels, specifically Wheel of Time series by Robert Jordan.

Why I'm Here:

I have always thought of myself as a life long learner and that has always been true in my life as an educator but I have decided to try to challenge myself further. Education is amazing but I also like the idea of putting something in and getting an immediate result, even if it's not quite what you intended. (exhibit A my very 1992 about me page) I'm excited to start this new challenge and continue to learn more.
